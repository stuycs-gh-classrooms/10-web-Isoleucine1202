Olympiads

The United States Biology Olympiad (USABO) stands as a premier biology competition in the United States, renowned for its rigor and dedication to fostering young scientific talent. This annual event challenges high school students with a passion for biology to showcase their knowledge, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills on a national stage. Through a series of exams, including a challenging open exam and a practical portion, participants delve into the depths of biological sciences, covering topics ranging from cellular biology to ecology.

Many olympiads are held globally, including the USABO and others, and typically lead up to team selection for the International Biology Olympiad (IBO). The IBO is an annual competition for secondary school students, typically at the upper secondary or high school level, who are passionate about biology. The IBO aims to promote interest in biology and excellence in scientific knowledge among young students from around the world.


Founded in 1990, the IBO brings together talented young biologists from various countries to compete in a series of rigorous theoretical exams and practical tasks. The competition tests participants on a wide range of biology topics, including genetics, physiology, ecology, evolution, and biochemistry.

Each participating country selects a team of its top students through national qualifying exams to represent them at the IBO. During the event, students engage in theoretical exams that challenge their understanding of biological concepts, as well as practical exams that assess their skills in laboratory techniques, experimentation, and analysis.


The IBO not only fosters friendly competition but also provides a platform for students to interact with peers from diverse cultural backgrounds, exchange knowledge, and develop international friendships. It encourages participants to think critically, apply their scientific knowledge, and approach biological problems with creativity and innovation.

Winning medals or recognition at the International Biology Olympiad is a significant achievement for participants, often opening doors to opportunities in academia, research, and scientific careers. The IBO plays a vital role in nurturing the next generation of biologists and promoting international cooperation and understanding in the field of biology.
